Our Programs

Wellness Clinic

All small or large pets can get medications, checks, and vaccinations.

Dog Training Classes

Through certification good manners are taught to dogs.It is Science-based dog training practices. Classes meet Tuesday and Thursday evenings. Dogs who finish the course earn an AKC Canine Good Citizen certificate.

Humane Education

Kids are taught to be empathetic and kind to any creatures. Available for grades K-8. Contact us to schedule a visit.
